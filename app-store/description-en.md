## Description

Amida is the all-in-one business tool built for freelance musicians. Track your gigs, create professional invoices, scan receipts, and see your earnings — all in one place.

MANAGE YOUR GIGS

- Add gigs with dates, fees, venues, and notes
- Track status from tentative to paid
- Support for multi-day gigs and projects
- Attach contracts and documents
- Duplicate gigs to save time

PROFESSIONAL INVOICING

- Generate polished PDF invoices with your company logo
- Automatic invoice numbering
- Send invoices directly via email
- Payment reminders with one tap
- Reverse charge support for EU clients
- Invoices in Swedish or English

SMART EXPENSE TRACKING

- Scan receipts with AI — amounts and suppliers extracted automatically
- 11 expense categories (travel, meals, instruments, and more)
- Link expenses to specific gigs
- Export to CSV for your accountant

CALENDAR & SCHEDULING

- Visual calendar with color-coded gigs
- Sync to Apple Calendar, Google Calendar, or any app via iCal feed
- See your availability at a glance

INSIGHTS & ANALYTICS

- Revenue dashboard with daily rates and totals
- Top clients and best-paying gigs
- Year-over-year comparisons
- Track unpaid invoices and upcoming payments

TEAM SUPPORT

- Share clients, gig types, and positions with your team
- Invite team members to your company account
- Personal or shared gig visibility

BUILT FOR MUSICIANS

- Custom gig types (concert, recording, teaching, rehearsal)
- Orchestra positions (concertmaster, tutti, principal, etc.)
- Multi-currency support for international work

Free to get started. Upgrade to Pro for unlimited invoices, AI receipt scanning, and email delivery.
